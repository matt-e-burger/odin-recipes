Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Hard-boiled or hard-cooked eggs are boiled long enough for the yolk to solidify (about 10 minutes).

They can be eaten warm or cold.

Hard-boiled eggs are the basis for many dishes, such as egg salad, cobb salad and Scotch eggs, and may be further prepared as deviled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place eggs in water
  2. Bring water to a boil and turn off heat
  3. Let it sit covered for like 8 minutes
  4. Remove eggs and place them gently in ice water
  5. Once cooled, peel and enjoy
